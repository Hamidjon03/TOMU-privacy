Privacy Policy – TOMU

Effective Date: November 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TOMU (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the TOMU mobile application (“the App”) — a language-learning platform with AI speaking practice and monthly subscription options.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone number
  • First name
  • Last name
  • Gender

1.2 Information Collected During Use

  • Voice recordings (only when using speaking practice)
  • Learning progress and performance
  • Subscription and payment status

1.3 Device Information

  • Device ID

We do not use Firebase or collect analytics/crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide AI pronunciation feedback
  • Improve learning accuracy
  • Process subscription payments

SMS Usage

We use SMS only for:

  • Registration confirmation
  • Password reset verification

No marketing messages, no push notifications.

3. Microphone Access

  • Used only during speaking exercises
  • Audio is recorded only when you press the microphone button
  • No background recording
  • Audio is securely uploaded for AI processing

4. AI & Third-Party Services

To analyze your pronunciation, we share audio only with:

  • OpenAI – speech processing
  • Google Speech-to-Text API – converting voice to text

All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS/TLS. Personal data (name, phone number) is never shared.

5. Payments

Payments are processed through Payme (Uzbekistan).
We do not store card details. We only receive payment status (success/failure).
TOMU does not use Google Play Billing.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only:

  • Payment confirmation with Payme
  • Audio data with OpenAI & Google Speech-to-Text
  • Data required by law, if applicable

7. Data Retention

  • Voice recordings: stored up to 30 days, then automatically deleted
  • Account information: stored until you request deletion
  • Payment records: stored as required by law

8. Data Security

We use strong protection measures including HTTPS/TLS encrypted transfer, secure servers, and restricted access to stored audio files.

9. Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to your data
  • Data correction
  • Full account deletion (removes all progress and audio)

TOMU is not intended for children under 13. If we discover data from a child under 13, we delete it immediately.
